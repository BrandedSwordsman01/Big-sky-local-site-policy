Big Sky Local Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-06

Big Sky Local is a mobile app designed to help people explore Big Sky, Montana, including finding food, checking what is open, planning itineraries, and viewing transport information.

1. No account required

You can use Big Sky Local without creating an account or signing in. We do not require a personal user profile to browse or plan inside the app.

2. Information we collect

We keep data collection limited.

Big Sky Local may process:
- app usage events, such as app opens, feature usage, itinerary actions, premium prompt interactions, and other limited non-personal analytics events
- ride request details submitted through the transport form, including request ID, ride date, ride time, pickup area, dropoff area, passenger count, and source
- location permission data when you choose to use location-based features

We do not require personal profiles, account registration, or sign-up just to browse or plan in the app.

3. Ride request logging

If you use the transport request flow, Big Sky Local may log non-personal trip request details so partner referrals can be tracked. This logging is intended to help measure lead activity and partner usage.

Ride request logging does not store your phone number from the SMS app. The optional name entered in the transport form is included in the prefilled text message to the transport provider, but is not intended to be stored in our logging sheet.

4. Analytics

Big Sky Local may collect limited, non-personal usage analytics to understand how features are used and to improve reliability, planning flows, premium conversion, and product decisions.

These analytics are intended to be lightweight and are not used to build personal user profiles.

5. Location permission

If you grant location permission, Big Sky Local uses it to improve nearby and open-now recommendations. Location is used to support app functionality and is not required to browse the app.

6. External services and links

Big Sky Local may rely on third-party services or open third-party apps when you choose certain actions, including:
- Google Play Billing
- Google Play In-App Review
- Google Apps Script / Google Sheets
- GitHub-hosted content feeds
- phone, SMS, maps, email, or browser apps on your device

Your use of those third-party services may also be governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

7. Data storage

Some app data, such as itinerary information, saved plans, and temporary app state, may be stored locally on your device. Limited ride request and analytics data may also be sent to our backend tools for product operation, improvement, and partner tracking.

8. Data accuracy and app function

We work to keep Big Sky Local useful and current, but app content, listings, business hours, prices, specials, and partner information may change over time. The app may also rely on third-party services and public information sources. Important details should be confirmed directly with the relevant business or service provider when needed.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the app changes. The latest version should always be the version linked from the official app listing.
